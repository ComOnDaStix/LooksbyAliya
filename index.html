The Stylist

Hello gorgeous, here's the story behind the chair

I believe every head of hair is a canvas, and every client deserves to walk out feeling like the best version of themselves. What started as a love for color and craft grew into a calling: creating looks that are personal, dimensional, and unmistakably you.

From lived-in balayage and seamless blonding to bridal hair and full glam, I tailor every formula and every cut to the person in my chair, never a cookie-cutter look. Find me in the heart of downtown Milwaukee at Sevva Salon.
